About Us

We grew up gaming, and as we spent more time immersed in video games, we began repairing old consoles for fun. Over time, we realized there was a demand for console repairs and decided to start GameFixPro. Our goal is to bring old and new game consoles back to life, and provide top-notch services for gamers everywhere!
